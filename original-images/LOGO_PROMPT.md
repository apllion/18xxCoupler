# 18xxCoupler Logo Generation Prompt

## Style Guide

Stylized cartoon illustration in a warm, muted vintage palette. Dominant colors: olive green, warm brown, copper-orange, and dark teal with golden amber highlights. NOT steampunk — realistic 1850s–1860s railroad era aesthetic. Cel-shaded with subtle gradients, thick outlines, and a slightly weathered, aged look. Think vintage travel poster meets illustrated history book.

**This must match the style and coloring of the scene images used in the app** (compartment interiors, track builders, signal box, water tower, etc.). Same warm, earthy tones. Same painterly-but-bold illustration style.

## Content & Composition

A classic 1860s steam locomotive pulling three freight/cargo wagons along a diagonal railroad track, moving from bottom-left toward upper-right. The train occupies the full width of the image.

**Locomotive (front, left side):**
- A heavy, detailed 1860s-era steam engine with a prominent round boiler front, large headlamp, cowcatcher, and visible wheels with connecting rods
- Smoke stack with a slight puff of steam/smoke trailing behind
- Warm copper and brass details on the boiler, dark body

**Wagons (behind locomotive, 3 cars):**
- Three small freight/cargo wagons of decreasing size trailing behind
- Each wagon carries a glowing screen/tablet device (a modern touch — representing the digital coupler concept)
- The screens glow with a subtle warm amber light
- Wagons are simpler, wooden/iron construction

**Track:**
- Diagonal railroad track with wooden sleepers/ties running underneath the entire train
- Track runs from lower-left to upper-right at roughly 20-30° angle

**Background:**
- Transparent / no background (PNG with alpha channel)
- OR solid black `#1a1a1a` background — specify which works better

## Dimensions

**Aspect ratio: approximately 16:9 wide** (758 × 432 pixels in the original).
The image should be wider than tall, landscape orientation. The train fills the width.

## What NOT to do

- Do NOT use bright cartoon colors (no neon teal, no bright orange)
- Do NOT use steampunk aesthetics (no gears, no Victorian goggles)
- Do NOT add text or lettering to the image
- Do NOT add borders, frames, or decorative edges
- Do NOT make it square — it must be wider than tall
