# 18xxCoupler Scene-Image Generation Prompt

## Style Guide

Stylized cartoon illustration in a warm, muted vintage palette. Dominant colors: olive green, warm brown, copper-orange, and dark teal with golden amber highlights. NOT steampunk — realistic 1850s–1860s railroad era aesthetic. Cel-shaded with subtle gradients, thick outlines, and a slightly weathered, aged look. Think vintage travel poster meets illustrated history book.

**Color reference:** Olive green, warm brown, copper-orange, dark teal with golden amber highlights. The palette should harmonize with an existing logo that uses dark teal `#2d6b6e`, copper orange `#e8833a`, and gold `#f5c542` — but the icons are more painterly and realistic than the logo's cartoon style. Black background `#1a1a1a`.

**Format:** Generate all icons in a single image as a grid (4 columns × 4 rows). Each icon must be **square and the same size**. **No borders or frames around individual icons.** Solid **black background** fills the space between and around all icons. The bottom-right cell (position 12) should be **empty/black** — reserved for future use.
