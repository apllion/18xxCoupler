# 1867 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** $15,000
- **Starting Cash:** $420 (2p) / $420 (3p) / $315 (4p) / $252 (5p) / $210 (6p)
- **Capitalization:** Incremental, 20% float (majors); 100% (minors)
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10% (majors); 100% single (minors)
- **Sell Movement:** down per block (blocked by president share)
- **Sell After:** Corp must have operated
- **Unsold Dividends:** none (bank pool shares pay nothing)
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Half Pay:** Yes
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- 16 minors merge into 8 majors, then nationalize into CN
- Dedicated Merger Rounds between ORs (phases 3--7)
- Bid box auction for 5 privates ;(
- Multiplier trains: 2+2 and 5+5E (available from phase 8) ;(
- Phase 8 train discounts ($275--$500 off) ;(
- Sibling to 1861 ;(

## Issue / Redeem
Standard: issue at current price (price drops 1 per share issued), redeem at current price (price rises 1 per share redeemed).

## Loans
Corp bank loans. $50 face value, corp receives $45 ($5 origination fee). Fixed interest $5/loan/OR. Max loans: 2 (minors), 5 (majors), unlimited (CN national). No price movement on take/repay. Interest locked at OR set start ;(. Cannot pay → take more loans or nationalize ;(. Game-end: price moves left 2 per outstanding loan before scoring.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
- **Half pay:** no movement
- **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
- **Sell shares:** down per block (blocked by president share)
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
Bid box -- items in stacked columns, top of each column available.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x2 -> Merger Round (phases 3--7)
- Game ends on bank break (current OR) or final phase (one more full OR set)
