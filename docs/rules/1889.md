# 1889 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** ¥7,000
- **Starting Cash:** ¥420 (2p) / ¥420 (3p) / ¥420 (4p) / ¥390 (5p) / ¥390 (6p)
🤖 - **Capitalization:** Full, 50% float
🤖 - **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
🤖 - **Sell Movement:** down per share
🤖 - **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
🤖 - **Pool Limit:** 50%
🤖 - **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** yen

## Specialties
- Shikoku intro game (streamlined 1830)
- Must sell in blocks
- No emergency buy from other corps

## Stock Market Movement
🤖 - **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
🤖 - **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
🤖 - **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
🤖 - **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
🤖 Waterfall -- ascending price auction for privates in order.

## Round System
🤖 - SR -> OR x1 (phase 2)
🤖 - SR -> OR x2 (phases 3--4)
🤖 - SR -> OR x3 (phases 5--D)
