# 18Chesapeake — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** $8,000
- **Starting Cash:** $1,200 (2p) / $800 (3p) / $600 (4p) / $480 (5p) / $400 (6p)
🤖 - **Capitalization:** Full, 60% float
🤖 - **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
🤖 - **Sell Movement:** down per share
🤖 - **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
🤖 - **Pool Limit:** 50%
🤖 - **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- Intro 18xx (streamlined 1830)
- Cornelius Vanderbilt -- random president's certificate
- D-train trade-in discount of $200 (from 4/5/6)
- 2p variant: 30% president share, 7-share corps

## Stock Market Movement
🤖 - **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
🤖 - **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
🤖 - **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
🤖 - **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
🤖 Waterfall -- ascending price auction for privates in order.

## Round System
🤖 - SR -> OR x1 (phase 2)
🤖 - SR -> OR x2 (phases 3--4)
🤖 - SR -> OR x3 (phases 5--D)
