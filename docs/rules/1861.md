# 1861 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** ₽15,000
- **Starting Cash:** ₽420 (2p) / ₽420 (3p) / ₽315 (4p) / ₽252 (5p) / ₽210 (6p)
- **Capitalization:** Incremental, 20% float (majors); 100% (minors)
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10% (majors); 100% single (minors)
- **Sell Movement:** down per block (blocked by president share)
- **Sell After:** Corp must have operated
- **Unsold Dividends:** none (bank pool shares pay nothing)
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** roubles

## Specialties
- 16 minors merge into 8 majors, then nationalize into RSR
- Nationalization events at phases 4/6/8
- Phase 8 forces all remaining minors into RSR national
- Sibling to 1867 (same designer) ;(
- Optional 1D column market variant ;(

## Issue / Redeem
Standard: issue at current price (price drops 1 per share issued), redeem at current price (price rises 1 per share redeemed).

## Loans
Corp bank loans. $50 face value, corp receives $45 ($5 origination fee). Fixed interest $5/loan/OR. Max loans: 2 (minors), 5 (majors), unlimited (RSR national). No price movement on take/repay. Interest locked at OR set start ;(. Cannot pay → take more loans or nationalize ;(. Game-end: price moves left 1 per outstanding loan before scoring.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= price (national exempt)
- **Half pay:** no movement
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per sale (blocked by president share)
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Emergency Buy
Take corp loans ($50, receive $45). Nationalization if at max loans.

## Auction
Bid box -- items in stacked columns, top of each column available.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x2 (all phases)
- Game ends on bank break (current OR) or final phase (one more full OR set)

## Game End
Bank break (current OR). Final phase triggers one more full OR set.
