## Share Structure
20% president + 8×10% (majors); 100% single share (minors).

## Par Prices
Par from market column. Concession grants right to par a major.

## Issue / Redeem
Standard: issue at current price (price drops 1 per share issued), redeem at current price (price rises 1 per share redeemed). Switches to full capitalization at phase 6.

## Loans
PLAYER loans (emergency train purchase). 150% of shortfall (50% immediate interest, rounded up). Cannot buy shares or place bids while in debt. +50% compound interest at end of each SR if unpaid. Debt deducted from final score. Loans do not count against bank limit. Auto-trigger on emergency train shortfall ;(.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= 2× price (majors only, minors always right 1)
- **Half pay:** no movement
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Mergers
Majors acquire minors during ORs. Minor assets transfer to major.

## Emergency Buy
Player loan for shortfall (150% of amount). Cannot buy/bid while in debt.

## Auction
Bid box — items in stacked columns, top of each column available for bidding.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x1 (phase 1)
- SR -> OR x2 (phases 2--7)

## Game End
Bank break (current OR set). £12,000 bank.

## Train Events
L/2 train pairing (minor L → major 2). Rusting: 2→4, 3→6. Full cap at phase 6.
