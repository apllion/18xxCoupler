# 18GB — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** Unlimited
- **Starting Cash:** £375 (2p) / £330 (3p) / £330 (4p) / £320 (5p) / £305 (6p)
🤖 - **Capitalization:** Full, 40% float
🤖 - **Shares:** 40% president + 3x20% (5-share corps; converts to 10-share)
🤖 - **Sell Movement:** down per share
🤖 - **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
🤖 - **Pool Limit:** 100% (no limit)
🤖 - **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** pounds

## Specialties
🤖 - 5-share/10-share conversion
- 40% president certificate
- Unique blue tile phase between green and brown
- X-trains (4X/5X/6X) replace conventional late-game trains
- Great Britain setting

## Stock Market Movement
🤖 - **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
🤖 - **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
🤖 - **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
🤖 - **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
🤖 Waterfall -- ascending price auction for privates in order.

## Round System
🤖 - SR -> OR x2 (all phases)
🤖 - Train counts scale by player count
