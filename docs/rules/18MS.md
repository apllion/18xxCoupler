# 18MS — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--4
- **Bank:** $10,000
- **Starting Cash:** $900 (2p) / $625 (3p) / $525 (4p)
- **Capitalization:** Full, 60% float
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
- **Max Ownership:** 70%
- **Sell Movement:** down per share
- **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- Draft privates (not auction) ;(
- Salvage train values (2+=$20, 3+=$30, 4+=$60)
- Multiplier D-trains (2D/4D/5D multiply revenue x2) ;(
- 70% max player ownership ;(
- Fixed round game end ;(

## Share Structure
20% president + 8×10% (standard 10-share).

## Par Prices
Par from market row.

## Issue / Redeem
No issue/redeem. Full capitalization.

## Loans
PLAYER loans (emergency train purchase). 50% interest applied immediately. Debts auto-repaid when player receives money. Player in debt cannot spend. Debt compounds +50% at end of each SR if unpaid. Debt deducted from final score.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= price
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Mergers
No mergers.

## Emergency Buy
Player takes loan for shortfall (50% interest). Auto-repay from income. Cannot spend while in debt.

## Auction
Draft — each player picks one private in turn order.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x2 (all phases)
- Fixed OR count: 2 per phase

## Game End
Fixed round count.

## Train Events
Salvage values: 2+=$20, 3+=$30, 4+=$60. D-trains: 2D/4D/5D multiply revenue.
