# 18SJ — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** kr10,000
- **Starting Cash:** kr800 (2p) / kr800 (3p) / kr600 (4p) / kr480 (5p) / kr400 (6p)
🤖 - **Capitalization:** Incremental (switches to full at phase 5), 60% float
🤖 - **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
🤖 - **Sell Movement:** down per share
🤖 - **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
🤖 - **Pool Limit:** 50%
🤖 - **Sell Order:** sell/buy/sell
- **Currency:** kr (kronor)

## Specialties
- Swedish railways setting
🤖 - Cap switches from incremental to full at phase 5
- Sell/buy/sell stock round order (like 1830)
🤖 - Nationalization events at phases 4/6/D
- Bonus tokens (Gota kanal, Sveabolaget port)
- KHJ minor splits revenue with owner
- D-train has $300 trade-in discount (from 4/5/6)
- E-train variant at kr1,300

## Stock Market Movement
🤖 - **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
🤖 - **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
🤖 - **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
🤖 - **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
🤖 English auction -- ascending bids on each private.

## Round System
🤖 - SR -> OR x1 (phase 2)
🤖 - SR -> OR x2 (phases 3--4)
🤖 - SR -> OR x3 (phases 5--E)
🤖 - Game ends on bankruptcy, stock ceiling, or bank break
