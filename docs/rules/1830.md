# 1830 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--6
- **Bank:** $12,000
- **Starting Cash:** $1,200 (2p) / $800 (3p) / $600 (4p) / $480 (5p) / $400 (6p)
- **Capitalization:** Full, 60% float
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
- **Sell Movement:** down per share
- **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy/sell
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- Classic 18xx baseline ;(
- D-train discounts ($300 trade-in from 4/5/6) ;(
- Sell/buy/sell stock round order ;(

## Issue / Redeem
No issue/redeem. Full capitalization.

## Loans
No loans.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= price
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Emergency Buy
President sells shares then contributes cash. Bankruptcy ends game immediately.

## Auction
Waterfall -- ascending price auction for privates in order.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x1 (phase 2)
- SR -> OR x2 (phases 3--4)
- SR -> OR x3 (phases 5--D)

## Game End
Bankruptcy ends game immediately. Bank break ends after current OR set.
