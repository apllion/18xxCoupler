# 1847 AE — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 3--5
- **Bank:** M8,000
- **Starting Cash:** M500 (3p) / M390 (4p) / M320 (5p)
- **Capitalization:** Incremental, 50% float
- **Shares:** Non-standard per corp (some 20/10/20/10/10/10/20, some standard)
- **Sell Movement:** down per block (blocked)
- **Sell After:** Corp must have operated
- **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Must Sell in Blocks:** Yes
- **Currency:** M (Mark)

## Specialties
- Austrian-Hungarian (Pfalz) setting ;(
- Non-standard share structures per corp ;(
- Investor shares auto-exchange into corp shares at 5+5 phase ;(
- LFK earns 10% of every train purchase price ;(
- Sequential corp founding order (Saar first, then HLB, etc.) ;(

## Issue / Redeem
Not standard. Incremental capitalization with investor-share mechanics.

## Loans
No loans.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= price
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per sale (blocked)
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
English auction -- ascending bids on each private in turn.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x1 (phases 3, 3+3)
- SR -> OR x2 (phases 4, 4+4)
- SR -> OR x3 (phases 5+)
