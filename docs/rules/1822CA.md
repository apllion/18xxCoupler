# 1822CA — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--7
- **Bank:** $12,000
- **Starting Cash:** $1,000 (2p) / $700 (3p) / $525 (4p) / $420 (5p) / $350 (6p) / $300 (7p)
- **Capitalization:** Incremental (full at phase 6), 20% float
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10% (majors); 100% single share (minors)
- **Sell Movement:** down per share
- **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Half Pay:** Yes
- **Must Sell in Blocks:** Yes
- **SR Order:** Most cash
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- 1822 system adapted for Canada ;(
- 30 minors + 10 majors with acquisition mergers
- Bid box auction for concessions, privates, and minors ;(
- Grain trains deliver grain from elevators to port cities ;(
- Eastern/Western Regional Scenario variants ;(

## Issue / Redeem
Standard: issue at current price (price drops 1 per share issued), redeem at current price (price rises 1 per share redeemed). Switches to full capitalization at phase 6.

## Loans
PLAYER loans (emergency train purchase). Same system as 1822: 150% of shortfall, +50%/SR compound, cannot buy/bid while in debt. Auto-trigger on emergency train shortfall ;(.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1; right 2 if revenue >= 2× price (majors only)
- **Half pay:** no movement
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Emergency Buy
Player loan for shortfall (150% of amount). Same as 1822.

## Auction
Bid box -- items in stacked columns, top of each column available for bidding.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x1 (phase 1)
- SR -> OR x2 (phases 2--7)

## Game End
Bank break. $12,000 bank.
