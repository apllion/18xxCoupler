# 1862 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--8
- **Bank:** £15,000
- **Starting Cash:** £1,200 (2p) / £800 (3p) / £600 (4p) / £480 (5p) / £400 (6p) / £345 (7p) / £300 (8p)
🤖 - **Capitalization:** Full, 50% float
🤖 - **Shares:** 30% president + 7x10%
🤖 - **Sell Movement:** down per share
🤖 - **Sell After:** After one full round
🤖 - **Unsold Dividends:** market -> corp
🤖 - **Pool Limit:** 50%
🤖 - **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Currency:** pounds

## Specialties
- Railway mania in Eastern Counties England
- 20 corporations with 30% president share
🤖 - Peer mergers: two equal corps combine, survivor gets averaged price
- All trains run unlimited distance, value determined by letter (A-H)
- Charter auction (no private companies)
- H-train triggers LNER formation

## Stock Market Movement
🤖 - **Pay dividend:** right 1 (up if at edge)
🤖 - **Withhold:** left 1 (down if at edge)
🤖 - **Sell shares:** down 1 per share sold
🤖 - **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Auction
🤖 Charter auction -- corps auctioned individually.

## Round System
🤖 - SR -> OR x1 (phase A)
🤖 - SR -> OR x2 (phases B--C)
🤖 - SR -> OR x3 (phases D--H)

## Corporations

## Private Companies
None -- corps are distributed via charter auction.

## Merger
🤖 Peer merger during OR. Two same-size corps merge; survivor price = survivor + (non-survivor / 2). Need 30%+ to be president after merge.
