# 1846 — Rules Reminder

## Quick Reference
- **Players:** 2--5
- **Bank:** $7,000 (2p) / $6,500 (3p) / $7,500 (4p) / $9,000 (5p)
- **Starting Cash:** $600 (2p) / $400 (3p) / $400 (4p) / $400 (5p)
- **Capitalization:** Incremental, 20% float
- **Shares:** 20% president + 8x10%
- **Sell Movement:** left (blocked by president share)
- **Unsold Dividends:** IPO -> corp
- **Pool Limit:** 50%
- **Sell Order:** sell/buy
- **Half Pay:** Yes
- **Currency:** $

## Specialties
- Secret draft (privates, minors, pass cards) ;(
- Issue/redeem shares (1846 style)
- Minors: MS and BIG4 ;(
- Train variants: 3/5, 4/6, 7/8 ;(

## Share Structure
20% president + 8×10% (standard 10-share).

## Par Prices
Par prices on market row. 20% float (president share only).

## Issue / Redeem
1846-style: issue shares at current price - 1 column, redeem at current price + 1 column. No stock price movement from issue/redeem. Corps may issue to raise emergency train funds.

## Loans
No loans.

## Stock Market Movement
- **Pay dividend:** right 1 if revenue >= price; right 2 if >= 2×; right 3 if >= 3× (and price >= $165)
- **Half pay:** left 1 if revenue < half price; else no movement
- **Withhold:** left 1
- **Sell shares:** left 1 (blocked by president share in market)
- **Sold out:** up 1 at end of SR

## Mergers
No mergers.

## Emergency Buy
President contributes cash. May issue shares first to raise funds.

## Auction
Secret draft — each player picks one item from a visible pool; choices revealed at end.

## Round System
- SR -> OR x2 (all phases)
- Cert limit varies by player count AND corp count

## Game End
Bank break ends after current OR set. Bankruptcy ends game.

## Train Events
Train variants: 3/5, 4/6, 7/8. Rusting: 2→6, 2 obsolete at 5. Privates close at phase III.
